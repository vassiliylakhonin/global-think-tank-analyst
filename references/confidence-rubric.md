# Confidence Rubric (Scored)

Use this rubric before final confidence label.

## Score dimensions (0-2 each)

1. **Source quality**
- 0: weak/unknown sources
- 1: mixed reliability
- 2: credible, recent, corroborated

2. **Cross-source agreement**
- 0: major contradictions
- 1: partial agreement
- 2: strong agreement

3. **Uncertainty exposure**
- 0: unknowns dominate
- 1: manageable uncertainty
- 2: uncertainty bounded with clear assumptions

4. **Model stress-test resilience**
- 0: fails under red-team challenge
- 1: partially resilient
- 2: resilient with minor caveats

## Mapping

- **0-2**: Speculative
- **3-4**: Low
- **5-6**: Medium
- **7-8**: High

Always include 1-line reason for the score.
